Beatles' discography

  
  by Yrjö Heinonen
A Beatles' discography, compiled by Yrjö Heinonen, on the basis of the data given in Mark Lewisohn's book on the Beatles' recording sessions (1988). The list contains all the records the Beatles officially released in England and the USA during 1962-70. The chronology of the discography is based on the order of release. All British singles as well as LP and EP releases containing new material are expressed by bold type.
